Coding has become my life! Now to explain the steps that lead to it. About a year ago, I was at my job, feeling underutilized and for a lack of better word, bored. I wanted to feel more challenged in my profession and I began to look for a possible career change where I felt I would be able to thrive. I started to look into programming. Started studying independently whenever I wasn't working at my full time job. Started off learning the basics of HTML/CSS, Javascripts, Ruby, and other frameworks. I had fallen in love! Fast forward 1 year, and now I am set to attend Dev Bootcamp and get the full DBC experience! Another step close to achieving my goal to be a programmer!
