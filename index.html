Thanks for visiting! Site is still under construction at the moment but feel free to look around! - 8/12/15

I had been working at an Aerospace MFR company for the past 7 years and I was starting to grow weary. Felt stuck. Every day it was the same thing over and over again. That repetition. Felt unchallenged and bored. Needed a change!

Friend suggested I look into programming to see if I would like it. Started to learn the basics of a couple languages and their frameworks. Started doing nothing else but learning how to code. I was hooked!

Fast forward a few months, and I'm about to attend a programming bootcamp to hone my skills in what could be the biggest decision of my life. Nervous and excited. Big changes! Dreaming big!
