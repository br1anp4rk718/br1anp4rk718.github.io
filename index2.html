Updates!

Thanks for visiting! Site is still under construction at the moment but feel free to look around! -August 12th 2015

I was working at an Aerospace MFR company for the past 7 years and I was on the verge of going insane. Every day it was the same thing over and over again. That repetition. Felt unstimulated and bored. Needed a change.

Friend suggested I look into programming to see if I would like it. Started to learn the basics of a couple languages and their frameworks. Started doing nothing else but learning how to code. I was hooked.

Fast forward a few months, and now I'm about to attend a programming bootcamp to hone my skills in what could be the biggest decision of my life. Nervous and excited. Big changes! Dreaming big!
